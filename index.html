Biography

I am a senior undergraduate student in Cyber Science and Engineering (CSE), Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I worked as a research assistant at Machine Vision and Intelligence Group (MVIG), led by Prof. Cewu Lu. My research interests involve Machine Learning, Security and Computer Vision.

Previously, I was very fortunate to work as a research intern at UC Berkeley and UIUC, working with Prof. Bo Li and Prof. Dawn Song. During the internship, I did some explorations on adversarial machine learning, collaborating with Prof. Yang Liu and Dr. Ruoxi Jia.

I am going to join Machine Learning Group, CS Department, University of Toronto. My advisors are Prof. Raquel Urtasun and Prof. Richard Zemel. Start a new journey!
